# Name

- The app is called Sports-Finder.

# Users

- Users want to find local pick-up sports games happening in their area

# Value proposition

A street-view map that highlights local games currently happening

# Key features

Simple mobile-friendly one-screen design with the app name at the top, and below it:

- street-view map with different icons in different locations to represent games that are happening
- 'Start Game' button on the bottom of the screen
  Simple operations:
- Tap an icon to see what is game is being played and where. Also shows the competitive level and who else is playing
- Tap a name to see their profile and past games.
- Press directions to see directions towards the game and be added to the list of players
- Press 'Start Game' button to set an icon at your location for your game.
  Recording-keeping:
- After selecting a game and pressing directions, log the game as one that the player has attended

# Example scenario

Here is an example session.

- Alice, Bob, Cathy, and Dave like to play basketball.
- Alice, Cathy, and Dave meet to play a game, but want to add a fourth to even out the teams.
- Alice starts the app on her phone.
- It shows a map of her current location.
- She presses 'Start Game' and fills out the information about the game, saying that it is a casual 2-on-2 basketball game.
- Bob check the app and sees the game pop up
- Wanting to check it out, Bob presses on the icon and then presses on Cathy's name to view her previous games
- Wanting to join, Bob presses 'Directions'
